Key Facts

Probes 7
Drone complement 100
First Probe launch 1st January 2001
Coordinated arrival 18th June 2021

Autonomous probes each hosting a Mission Personality™ coordinating a drone survey of bioforms on seven of the Kepler planets

Five things to know

  1. Simultaneously surveys are running across seven exoplanets
  2. Trained alongside human naturalists, the onboard AI directs all operations
  3. Survey drones are loaded with one of four different image recognition algorithms
  4. Data transmission rates are low. Early reports only include data on bioform types, colouration and behaviour
  5. Colour can help us infer a great deal about an ecosystem

The Drones
